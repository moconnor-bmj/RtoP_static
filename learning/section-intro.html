ECG skills: basic anatomy and physiology of the heart

Learning outcomes

Upon completion of this module you should understand:

  • The structure of the heart
  • The blood flow through the heart
  • What causes the heart to beat.

Written by:

Kevin Channer

Kevin Channer

Kevin Channer

Biography :

Kevin Channer was appointed in 1989 as a general physician and cardiologist to the Royal Hallamshire Hospital, part of Sheffield Teaching Hospitals NHS Trust. He was clinical director of medicine at the Royal Hallamshire Hospital from 1994 to 1998 and deputy clinical director until April 2002. He was head of the postgraduate school of medicine for the Yorkshire Deanery (South) from 2007 to 2012.

Biography :

He is an honorary clinical senior lecturer in medicine for the University of Sheffield Medical School, and was appointed Honorary Professor of Cardiovascular Medicine at Sheffield Hallam University in July 2004. Since he retired from the NHS in 2012 he has been appointed to the Council of the Medical Defence Union (MDU) and continues to provide expert advice for HM coroners, and the Court in both criminal and civil cases.

Heather Wetherell

Heather Wetherell

Biography :

Heather Wetherell is a GP partner in Middlesbrough. She joined her current practice 20 years ago and is now senior partner. She also works as a GP with specialist interests in Cardiology, at the James Cook University Hospital. In 2012 she founded the very popular #ECGclass on Twitter and she now tweets, and blogs, on a regular basis.
