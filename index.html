Research to Publication

Self study courses on clinical research methods and publication

Research to Publication is an eLearning product brought to you by The British Medical Journal in partnership with University of San Francisco California). These course are dsigned to help doctors in emerging economies develop their research expertise by providing a gold standard online programme of self study courses on clinical research methods and publication.
